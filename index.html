The Philosophy

Lumière is born from the belief that dining is the ultimate sensory art form. We don't just serve food; we curate moments of light, texture, and taste.

Our Executive Chef Julian Marc combines classical French discipline with the avant-garde spirit of Tokyo’s nightlife, resulting in a menu that is as visually stunning as it is delicious.
